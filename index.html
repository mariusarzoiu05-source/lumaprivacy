At Luma, your privacy and emotional safety are our top priorities. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize your personal information — ever.
Section 01
Information We Collect

Luma collects only the data necessary to provide and improve the experience:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, first name, and last name. This information is securely stored in cloud services to enable authentication, account management, and synchronization across devices.
  • App data: Mood check-ins, journal entries, and progress data may be stored locally on your device and/or securely synchronized to cloud storage if you are logged into an account.
  • Anonymous analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage data may be collected to improve features and app performance.
Section 02
How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the application:

  • Account authentication and access
  • Saving and synchronizing journal entries and progress
  • Improving user experience and application performance
We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize your personal information.
Section 03
Cloud Storage & Security

Account information (email, first name, last name) is securely stored in trusted cloud infrastructure providers for authentication and synchronization purposes.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Section 04
Data Deletion & Account Removal

You may request deletion of your account and all associated cloud-stored data at any time by contacting us via email.

Upon verified request, your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.

If you are using the app without an account, uninstalling the application will remove all locally stored data from your device.

To request account deletion, contact: nextbytestudioscontact@gmail.com
Section 05
Children's Privacy

Luma is intended for general audiences but is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Section 06
Safety and Crisis Support

Luma is a self-reflection and wellbeing companion and not a medical or emergency service.

If you feel unsafe or are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services immediately — 112 in EU / 911 in US.
Section 07
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Section 08
Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to us directly.
